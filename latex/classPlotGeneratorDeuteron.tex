\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Facilitates the creation of plots to visualize momentum distributions of nucleons within a deuteron. 

This class is designed to generate graphical representations for the momentum distributions of nucleons inside a deuteron, based on various nucleon-\/nucleon potential models.
